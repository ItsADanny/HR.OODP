Learning goals:

* for-loop with multiple conditions

Ask the user to enter the PIN code of their bank pass, 
which is 1234 (a string). The user has three attempts to get it correct.

Note: you MUST use a for-loop.

Example 1:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Enter your PIN
3 attempts left
4321
Enter your PIN
2 attempts left
2345
Enter your PIN
1 attempts left
5432
Your pass is confiscated.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Example 2:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Enter your PIN
3 attempts left
2345
Enter your PIN
2 attempts left
1234
Correct!
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
